Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence refers to machines designed to mimic human intelligence.

How does quantum computing work?

Quantum computing leverages quantum bits (qubits) to perform complex calculations at unprecedented speeds.

What are the benefits of 5G?

5G offers faster internet speeds, lower latency, and enhanced connectivity for smart devices.

How does blockchain ensure security?

Blockchain uses decentralized, encrypted ledgers to secure transactions and prevent tampering.

Is space tourism possible?

Yes, companies like SpaceX and Blue Origin are pioneering commercial space travel.

What are IoT devices?

IoT (Internet of Things) devices are interconnected smart gadgets that communicate and automate tasks.

What is the future of AI in healthcare?

AI is expected to enhance diagnostics, robotic surgeries, and personalized medicine.

How will technology impact jobs?

Automation and AI will transform industries, creating new job opportunities while replacing repetitive tasks.
